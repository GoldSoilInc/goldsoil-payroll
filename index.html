Payment Mode:

Type Legend — what each commission type means

Acquisitions (LAM)

LAM Advance
$50–$250 fixed payment at contract signing, tiered by Deal Spread. Only paid on contracts signed on/after Sep 2025.
LAM Retraction
Clawback of the advance when a contract is cancelled. Skipped if the LAM has separated (not chasing former employees).
LAM Final
Tiered rate (6%–10%) × Adj Gross Profit at close, minus any advance already paid. Floored at $0 on negative-AGP deals (no advance clawback).

Closing (LTC)

LTC Closing Bonus
Flat $50 per closed-and-funded deal. Currently all attributed to Pracy Ann Pryce (sole active LTC).

Comps Team (LPA / LLP)

LPA
0.3% × Adj Gross Profit for the Property Analyst on each closed deal. Flagged REVIEW pending Quality of Comps ≥95% verification.
LLP
0.15% × Adj Gross Profit for the Lead Processor. Same QoC ≥95% gate caveat.

Outreach — Component A (per-lead approval bonus)

Per-Lead (Phone Fully Q)
Split: $1 to LIA (Sourcer) + $4 to LIM (Qualifier). Same person did both → $5.
Per-Lead (Phone Semi Q)
Split: $1 to LIA + $2 to LIM. Same person → $3.
Per-Lead (Text Fully Q)
$1 to the LIA (text-only qualification — no LIM phone step).
Per-Lead (… → blank-Q)
Phone Fully/Semi Q but Qualifier field is blank → falls back to $1 LIA only. LIM portion forfeited (v4 §4A.1).

Outreach — Component B (closing share on funded deals)

LIA Closing
Sourcer's share of a 0.3% × AGP pool, split using the same proportions as the per-lead bonus for that lead category.
LIM Closing
Qualifier's share of the same pool. Blank Sourcer or Qualifier → that share is forfeited, not redistributed.

Sales (LPM)

LPM Portfolio
Matrix rate (0.375%–1.5%) × Σ(AGP) across all the LPM's closed listings for the period. Rate is determined by portfolio Avg DOM × Portfolio PAR. Negative portfolio AGP → floored to $0.

Management & Executive

Outreach Team Mgr Override
20% × sum of direct reports' Component A bonuses. Component B closing commissions are excluded from the override base.
NOI-Tiered Exec
Revenue-tier rate × monthly NOI. Currently applies to Art Oaing (DOA PH) and Leslie Bernolo (DOT) — both pending revision.
